01
Beyond Blood Sugar — Diabetes Health Indicators
Rggplot2tidyverseStatistical AnalysisData Viz

A data-driven deep dive into 250,000+ BRFSS health records exploring how body weight, socioeconomic inequalities, and national economic growth collectively shape global diabetes risk — going far beyond just blood sugar.

  • 80% of people with type 2 diabetes are overweight — BMI 25–35 is the critical risk zone.
  • Diabetes risk falls sharply as income and education level rise — low-income, low-education groups face the highest burden.
  • Higher GDP correlates with higher obesity rates, suggesting economic growth without lifestyle control increases metabolic risk.
  • Countries with higher obesity almost always have higher diabetes prevalence — strong positive relationship confirmed.
  • High blood pressure is ~5x more common in diabetic individuals vs. normal population.

02
Used Car Price Analytics — 550K Listings
TableauSQLPythonForecastingRegression

Three interactive Tableau dashboards built on 550K+ used car listings, helping a new auto dealer make data-driven decisions in a competitive market — from stock selection to pricing strategy.

  • Mercedes-Benz & BMW command highest avg prices ($20–22K) — luxury brand far outweighs condition differences.
  • Avg selling price: $9,431 across 9,149 cars with avg odometer of 94,588 miles.
  • High mileage sharply reduces value even in good condition — odometer is a critical pricing signal.
  • State pricing ranges from $2,800 to $15K — location, taxes, and local demand drive regional variance.
  • Market forecast shows used car selling prices trending toward $10B+ by 2040 — strong growth sector.
